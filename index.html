Frequently asked questions

Is it really free?

Yes. QuickImage is free and requires no account. If it helps you, you can support the project with a Ko-fi tip.

Are my images uploaded anywhere?

No. Compression and conversion happen in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to this website.

Which format should I choose?

WebP is usually the lightest. JPG works everywhere for photos. PNG is best when you need transparency or graphic detail.
